  • HIGH marks the top fraction of apparent worst cases given your dataset composition (default 20%). Tune with --high_q and --intermediate_q.
  • The composite score increases with lower identity/coverage, larger/denser indels, higher LCR/repeat content, and larger GC mismatch. It is partially mitigated by long identical AA blocks (good for BLAST seeding).
